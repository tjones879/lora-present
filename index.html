LoRa and Secure Decentralized Communication

Big Picture

There is a need for communication that is:

  • Secure
  • Decentralized
  • Long Range
  • Energy Efficient

Where I'm at

I have experience with traditional 915 MHz band transceivers and connecting them to embedded linux devices.
I have previously written secure android communication applications.

Final Product

A self-contained unit that is capable of:

  • interfacing with mobile devices and traditional systems running Linux.
  • maintaining a reliable and error-resilient communication up to a range of 5km LOS.
  • communicating on an open source stack.
  • allowing users to easily communicate via text or voice with multiple people simultaneously.
